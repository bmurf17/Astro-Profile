As the years draws to an end, I think I just want to reflect on what I've been able to build over this past year. Looking back, I had some fun with these side projects, learned a lot, and I have some cool thoughts on how I can move into next year. 
## Book Tracker Project

![Home Page](/Down-the-hall.PNG "Home Page")

### Overview/Thoughts

My main flagship project over 2025! It works mostly, I still have a couple of errors related to third party book providers, but it has been a long time since I've had to scramble to fix the app. I think I had a goal to get some users into this app, but I haven't taken the plunge to get people on the app. I still want to polish it up a little bit, but haven't put the time in. Maybe having people use it would let me know where to polish it, but I don't want to have people bounce off of it if I open them up to it and then boom they hate it. So not done with this one as I still have to fix some caching and all that jazz.

### 2026 Goals

These goals have remained pretty static over the year. I think I can keep it pretty small though as I mainly need to just knock out some small stuff with the potential to get this into an app store:

- Make Caching not suck on pages that aren't stats
- Mobile view clean up
- Spin up a mobile app
